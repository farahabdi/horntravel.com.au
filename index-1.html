About us

Horn Travel is a Melbourne based travel agency with offices in Melbourne and Adelaide.

We pride ourselves on getting to know all our customers and in learning specifically what you want from your holiday experiences and how we can make that a reality each and every time you travel with us. We are also available to assist you on each step of the holiday journey, from planning to booking to travelling to returning home and to booking again!

Horn Travel’s mission is to offer African travellers in Australia industry-leading service, access to the best value travel deals and unparalleled customer assistance and convenience.

Our core values

  • 1.
    Quality and service
    At Horn Travel, quality and service go side by side. We do our utmost to ensure that these two hallmarks are met at each step of your journey. To assist achieve these values, we carefully monitor client satisfaction and are constantly in search of new methods to exceed our clients’ expectations.
  • 2.
    A personal touch
    From very first contact, through the entire duration of your journey, and till you’re safely back home, we’re with you at every step. You will know your personal travel consultant, as we make it a necessary to know our clients personally and to go through the entire journey.
  • 3.
    Save time and effort
    Researching and organizing your individual holiday generally is a worrying job. Allow us to do that for you to get the results you want! By choosing our travel agency, you can save both time and money by booking your entire itinerary services directly with a specialised local agency.
